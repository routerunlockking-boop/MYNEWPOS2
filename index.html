Shipping & Returns Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Shipping Information

Smart Zone LK delivers to all 25 districts in Sri Lanka. We use reliable courier partners to ensure your products reach you safely and on time.

  • Colombo & Suburbs: 1-2 business days
  • Major Cities: 2-3 business days
  • Other Districts: 3-5 business days
  • Free Delivery: Orders above LKR 15,000
  • Standard Delivery: LKR 350 - LKR 650 (based on location)

Return Policy

We offer a 7-day return policy for defective products. Items must be in original packaging with all accessories and documentation.

  • Products must be unused and in original condition
  • Return shipping costs are covered for defective items
  • Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
  • Exchange available for same product if defective

Warranty Information

Last updated: January 2025

Manufacturer Warranty

All products sold by Smart Zone LK come with official manufacturer warranty. Warranty periods vary by product and brand.

Warranty Coverage

  • TP-Link: 2-3 years manufacturer warranty
  • ASUS: 2-3 years manufacturer warranty
  • Huawei: 1-2 years manufacturer warranty
  • Xiaomi: 1 year manufacturer warranty
  • Netgear: 1-2 years manufacturer warranty

What's Covered

Manufacturing defects, hardware malfunctions under normal use, and firmware issues are covered. Physical damage, water damage, and unauthorized modifications void the warranty.

Claim Process

Contact our support team via WhatsApp or email with your order number and issue description. We'll guide you through the warranty claim process.
